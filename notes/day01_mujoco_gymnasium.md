# Day 1: MuJoCo and Gymnasium-Robotics Basics

## What I learned

Gymnasium provides a standard RL interface with reset and step functions.

MuJoCo is a physics engine for robot simulation.

FetchReach-v3 is a simple robotic reaching task where a Fetch manipulator moves its end effector to a target position.

## Important concepts

- observation
- action space
- reward
- achieved_goal
- desired_goal
- is_success

## My first experiment

I ran random actions in FetchReach-v3 for 10 steps and printed observations, rewards, and success flags.
